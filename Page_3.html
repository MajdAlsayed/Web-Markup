Plan Your Meals and Control Portions to Reduce Waste

Meal planning and portion control are essential for minimizing food waste and saving money.

Middle Section

Card Container

Meal Planning Card

Why Meal Planning Helps Reduce Waste

Meal planning ensures you only buy what you need, reducing the chances of food spoiling.

  • Less Overbuying: You can create a shopping list based on planned meals, preventing impulse purchases and buying too much.
  • Reduces Spoilage: Meal planning helps you use ingredients across multiple meals, ensuring nothing goes to waste.
  • Money-Saving: By buying the right amount of food, you save money on groceries.

Meal Portioning Card

How to Plan Meals for the Week

Follow these steps to make meal planning simple and effective:

  • Create a Weekly Meal Plan: Take a few minutes each week to plan out your meals for each day.
  • Make a Shopping List: Create your shopping list based on the ingredients you'll need for your meal plan.
  • Portion Wisely: Portion out what is necessary for one meal, store the rest properly to use for other days by scheduling leftover meals.

Food Storage Card

Serve the Right Amount Every Time

Serving the right portion sizes helps ensure that food doesn’t get left on the plate and thrown away.

  • Start Small: Serve smaller portions, and allow for seconds if needed. This prevents food from being left uneaten.
  • Use Portion Guides: Use portion guides for the best idea of portioning food that minimizes food wastage.
  • Store Leftovers: If you have extra food left, store it in airtight containers in the fridge to prevent it from spoiling.
